Over the course of this simulation, I worked as part of Quantium's data science team on a real-world retail analytics problem — here's what I got to do:

📊 Data preparation & customer analytics — cleaned and analysed transaction datasets to uncover purchasing patterns and segment customers by lifestage and spending behaviour.

🏪 Uplift testing & control store selection — used correlation and magnitude distance scoring to identify benchmark stores, then applied statistical testing to evaluate the impact of trial store layouts on sales and customer count.

📋 Commercial insights & reporting — synthesised findings into a structured report for the Category Manager, following the Pyramid Principle framework to deliver clear, evidence-based recommendations.

Key takeaway: data is only as valuable as the story you tell with it. Translating numbers into actionable commercial decisions is where the real impact happens.
